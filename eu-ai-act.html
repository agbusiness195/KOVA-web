EU AI Act Compliance Guide

By August 2026, Europe requires AI systems to be transparent and accountable. Penalties can reach €35 million or 7% of global revenue. This guide explains how Kova helps you meet those requirements — in plain language.

EU AI Act Requirement

Art. 12 · Risk management

Ongoing identification and mitigation of risks throughout the lifecycle.

Art. 13 · Data governance

Training and validation data quality and relevance.

Art. 14 · Technical documentation

Documentation of design, development, and operation.

Art. 15 · Record-keeping

Automatic logging of events and decisions.

Art. 17 · Transparency

Human oversight and interpretability.

How Kova Helps

Agents declare their rules. You can verify they followed them without seeing how they work.

Rules are linked to data handling. You can trace what was allowed.

Every action produces a record. Technical evidence is built in.

Automatic logging of what happened and why. No manual tracking.

Third parties can verify compliance. Human oversight is supported.
